Part IX

The Future I See

I absolutely love your smile. It brings dead parts of me back to life.

I would do anything, small gestures, silly dances, just to see you laugh.

I imagine us sitting on a bench by the beach... It's cold. You're sitting on my lap facing me. We share a blanket. We talk all night. And maybe I cry. Because crying in front of the right person is strength.

What I want

I want to watch you heal slowly. Not because I pressure you. But because I want to love you so deeply that you start loving yourself fully.

I want us to grow emotionally. Career-wise. As people.

I want our video call smiles to never fade.
