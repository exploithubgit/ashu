Chapter Two

Where I Was Wrong & The Fear I Felt

I rushed us.

I should have worked on myself more. I should have built us slower. I should have opened up fully instead of protecting parts of myself.

When you went quiet for days because you were going through a hard time, my mind didn't think, "She's struggling."

The Thought That Haunted Me

It thought, "She lost interest." It thought, "I ruined this." It thought, "I'm about to lose her."

So that morning I sent that message saying maybe we should break up. Not because I wanted to leave. But because I was scared.

Scared of being abandoned. Scared of being alone again. Scared of losing the one person who made me feel like myself.

When you replied the next day, especially when you said you were glad I double texted, something inside me calmed down.

In 23 years of my life, no one had ever made me feel as safe to be myself as you did.
