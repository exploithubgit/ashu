What I Promise You

But I've learned something:

You are not a child. You are strong. If you need help, you will ask.

I'll be there. In the middle of the storm. In the middle of the night. Whenever you need me.

If just my presence is enough, then I will give you that.

A lots of forehead kisses for you,

Forehead kisses aren't romance to me.

They are promises.

And I don't promise what I can't fulfill.
