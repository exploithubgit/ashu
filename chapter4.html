What We Shared & What Changed Me

After we patched things up, we both opened up deeply.

You Trusted Me

Your darkest experiences. Your trauma. Your years of pain. Your friend's death. The dark place you were in before me. I wasn't burdened. I was honored.

Then I told you about my gynecomastia. The embarrassment. The ragging. The teenage shame. The years of hiding.

I cried telling you that. But it felt freeing. Because for the first time, I wasn't alone inside my own story.

You made vulnerability feel safe.

I am not the worthless piece of shit I sometimes believed I was. I have potential. I have character. I have charm. I have depth. Your safety revealed parts of me that were always there but buried.
